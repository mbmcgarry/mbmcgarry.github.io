My research interests

... focus on the scientific pursuit of a safe, emissions-free, sustainable, high-output energy source. Particularly, I am interested in the promise of a closed nuclear fuel cycle, nuclear repository performance, and investigations of safety. My tool of choice in these endeavors is scientific computing.

If you are interested in such past projects please read my CV or check out my publications.
